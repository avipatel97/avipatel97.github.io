hello. i'm avi patel

i'm a medical student, creator, and human in Boston, MA. i study medicine at Tufts University with a particular interest in emergency medicine and the intersection of technology and life. i also am a novice coder, seasoned ui/ux tester, avid hiker, space fanboy, film enthusiast, and explorer by hobby.
